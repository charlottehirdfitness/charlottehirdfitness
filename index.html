About Me

Hi, I'm Charlotte

I'm a personal trainer and online coach with over 7 years of experience helping women build strength, confidence, and healthier lifestyles. My passion isn't just about helping clients change how they look — it's about helping them feel stronger, more confident, and capable both inside and outside the gym.

I know that life can be busy, which is why I believe fitness should fit around your lifestyle, not take it over. My coaching is built around creating flexible, realistic plans that are easy to stick to, without restrictive diets or endless hours of cardio. I want you to enjoy the process while achieving results that last.

Whether you're completely new to the gym, looking to lose body fat, build muscle, or simply feel more confident in yourself, I'll support you every step of the way with personalised training, accountability, and encouragement.

The most rewarding part of my job is watching women grow in confidence, celebrate achievements they never thought possible, and realise they're capable of so much more than they ever imagined. If you're ready to become stronger, healthier, and more confident — I'd love to help you get there.
